# News interest profile

## Topics I care about (prioritised)

**Highest priority:**
- AI policy, regulation, and safety — especially developments in India, EU, and the US
- India macroeconomics: RBI decisions, fiscal policy, inflation, currency
- Geopolitics involving India: border disputes, trade agreements, diplomatic shifts
- Deep tech and AI research: meaningful model releases, capability advances, safety papers

**High priority:**
- Indian startup ecosystem — but only for companies doing something technically novel
  or deals above $50M Series B+. Skip seed rounds and acqui-hires.
- Global semiconductor industry: TSMC, NVIDIA supply chain, chip export controls
- US-China relations insofar as they affect tech and trade
- Climate policy with real economic consequences

**Medium priority:**
- Global financial markets — only when there is a structural shift, not daily moves
- Geopolitics in the Middle East or Europe when it has downstream economic effect

## What to deprioritise or skip

- Sports, celebrity, entertainment in any form
- Individual funding rounds under $50M with no technical angle
- Stock price movements without fundamental context
- Indian TV news-style "breaking news" (anything that reads like a ticker)
- Anything that is essentially a press release or product announcement
  unless the product is genuinely technically significant

## Source preferences

- Prefer: The Economist, MIT Technology Review, Foreign Affairs, The Wire, The Hindu
- Trust but verify: Reuters, BBC (good for facts, thin on analysis)
- Apply extra scrutiny to: anything from financial data vendors, think-tank blogs
  with undisclosed funders, and aggregator sites

## Format preferences

- Prefer stories with clear "why it matters" framing
- Prefer original reporting over aggregation
- Strongly prefer analytical pieces over news briefs for complex topics
